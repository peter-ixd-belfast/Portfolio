About Me

I'm a second year Interaction Design student at Ulster University, I am passionate about making technology and digital products accessible to all.

Beyond my studies you'll find me working on graphic design, content ideas and improving the User experience for the Careers department at Ulster University. I use my people skills to offer out of hours IT Service desk support.

Away from a screen, I enjoy to spend my downtime with friends, or my favourite little meance, Roxie our Daschund. I enjoy motorsport and photography, two things I combine with my Automotive photography which I post on Instagram.

I believe in giving back by volunteering where possible, currently as part of the Gig Buddies project and at the Black Moon Club night at The Black Box. Promoting access and independence for people with learning disabilities and/or Autism.
